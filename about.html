A modern and innovative approach to entertainment.

We're known for our unwavering commitment to crafting unforgettable and unique experiences, bringing technology and the social scene together.


At VisionVR, we are redefining entertainment by merging cutting-edge virtual reality with classic arcade fun and social dining. Founded in Austin, Texas, we’re dedicated to creating immersive, unforgettable experiences for gamers and non-gamers alike.

Our mission is to make high-quality VR accessible to everyone, offering a space where technology, creativity, and community come together. From exclusive proprietary games to a welcoming café, VisionVR is more than an arcade—it’s a destination.

We believe in innovation and inclusivity, designing experiences that connect people of all ages and backgrounds. Whether you’re here for thrilling solo adventures or group events, VisionVR is your gateway to endless possibilities.
